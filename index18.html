    "Friesa" is a web design project with a focus on social functions. The goal of the project is to create a platform that brings people together to share their interests and connect with others who have similar passions. The website has a clean and modern design, with user-friendly features that make it easy for users to navigate and interact with the community.

    About

    Working within a major automotive holding, I acted as the key visual designer for a diverse portfolio of 15+ international brands. My role required adapting global advertising standards to the local market while maintaining strict brand consistency.

    I delivered high-performance creatives for European giants like Renault, Peugeot, Citroën, DS, and Skoda, as well as Asian market leaders including Hyundai, Kia, Nissan, Mitsubishi, and Infiniti.

    My scope also covered the rapidly growing sector of new automotive players such as Chery, Jetour, and MG, ensuring their launch campaigns resonated with new audiences. Additionally, I supported service-oriented visual communication for Bosch Service.

    Tasks

    1. Brand Book Expertise:

      • Brand Book Expertise: In-depth study and analysis of technical brand books for each manufacturer. I mastered the specific design codes—from typography rules to color palettes—to ensure that every creative asset was 100% compliant with the strict global standards of brands like Renault, Bosch, and Infinity.


    2. Visual Identity Management:

      • Managing distinct visual styles for over 15 manufacturers simultaneously. I seamlessly switched between the luxury minimalism of DS & Infiniti, the technological focus of Hyundai & Nissan, and the practical utility of Ford & Renault.


    3. Sales Campaigns:

      • Designing conversion-focused assets for seasonal sales, new model launches (e.g., Jetour, MG), and service offers. Cross-Platform Design: Producing unified visuals for social media, digital display ads, and offline showroom materials.

    - User-friendly interface: The web design for our online cinema is designed with ease-of-use in mind. Our interface is intuitive and simple, allowing users to easily navigate the site and find the movies they want to watch.

    - Seamless browsing experience: We understand that users want to spend less time searching for movies and more time watching them. Our web design ensures a seamless browsing experience, with quick-loading pages and easy access to our movie catalog.


    - Mobile responsive design: With more and more people accessing websites on their mobile devices, our web design is mobile responsive, ensuring that users can easily access our site on any device.

    - Personalized recommendations: Our web design includes a personalized recommendation system that suggests movies based on users' viewing history and preferences. This makes it easier for users to find movies that they will love.


    - Comprehensive search functionality: Our web design includes a comprehensive search functionality that allows users to search for movies based on genre, actor, director, and other criteria. This makes it easy for users to find exactly what they are looking for.
