    About

    "Model.kiev" is an impressive web platform designed to revolutionize the process of finding models for various activities, such as photo shoots and other related endeavors. With its intuitive interface and comprehensive features, this project aims to create a seamless and immersive online experience for users in search of the perfect model. By showcasing an extensive database of models, complete with detailed information, stunning photographs, and a portfolio showcase, "Model.kiev" serves as a captivating demonstration of the platform's visually appealing design and exceptional functionality.

    TASKS

    1. Enhance User Experience (UX):

      • Enhance the intuitiveness and user-friendliness of navigation, enabling swift information retrieval for users. Improve the interaction with website elements to boost usability and minimize the occurrence of errors. Ensure rapid page loading and a seamless interface performance to amplify user satisfaction.

      • Craft an Appealing and Contemporary User Interface (UI): Design a website interface that exudes style and aesthetic allure, aligning perfectly with the premium Model Kiev identity. Incorporate contemporary design trends and elements to forge a distinct and unforgettable website visual.

    2. Boost Conversion Rates:

      • Strategize page layouts based on conversion-centric design principles, aiming to optimize successful user interactions such as orders, inquiries, and subscriptions. Reinforce visually captivating elements that seize attention and motivate users to undertake desired actions.

    3. Seamless Multichannel Experience:

      • Design a responsive and consistent user interface across various devices, ensuring a seamless experience from desktop to mobile.

    Target audience >


    The target audience for Model Kiev consists of individuals seeking exceptional model selection services. This demographic encompasses fashion enthusiasts, brand managers, and event organizers who are tech-savvy and aged 25-55. They have a keen eye for diverse and high-quality models and are eager to engage with a community centered around modeling and fashion. Model Kiev's target audience values efficiency in model selection, aesthetic appeal, and the opportunity to connect and collaborate within the fashion industry.
