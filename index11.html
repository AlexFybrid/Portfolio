    BISNESS SITE

    "Ekfood" is a carefully developed design layout and website for a well-known company specializing in dairy products. The website design reflects the company's core values, emphasizes transparency and demonstrates the excellent quality of the dairy products offered. Within the site, we tried to give visitors a complete picture of the company itself, telling about its history, mission and values. In addition, we have given considerable space to detailed information about the wide range of exceptional dairy products offered by the company. This site serves as a gateway for customers to the world of dairy excellence that Ekfood is proud to represent.

    About

    "Ekfood" is a carefully developed design layout and website for a well-known company specializing in dairy products. The website design reflects the company's core values, emphasizes transparency and demonstrates the excellent quality of the dairy products offered. Within the site, we tried to give visitors a complete picture of the company itself, telling about its history, mission and values. In addition, we have given considerable space to detailed information about the wide range of exceptional dairy products offered by the company. This site serves as a gateway for customers to the world of dairy excellence that Ekfood is proud to represent.

    TASKS


    1. User experience (UX) design:
      • Develop intuitive and user-friendly navigation that provides the user with quick access to the information they need.

    2. Create an attractive and modern user interface (UI):
      • Utilize current design trends and elements to create a unique and memorable look and feel for the site.

    3. Showcase and product information:
      • Create a compact and user-friendly storefront that showcases all of the company's products

    4. Optimization for all possible devices:
      • Develop a design that will be usable on both PC and mobile devices.

    Target audience

    People who are actively interested in product questions, aged 12 to 50, with access to modern mobile devices. Special attention is paid to those who are looking for detailed information about the product. The main goal is to raise brand awareness.
